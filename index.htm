Support your frameworks and libraries

Cover popular frameworks including Spring-MVC, Struts, Tapestry and many mores.

Integrate with your IDE

Plugins are available for Eclipse, IntelliJ and NetBeans. Command line integration is available with Ant and Maven.

Continous integration

Can be used with systems such as Jenkins and Sonar-Source.

OWASP TOP 10 and CWE coverage

Extensive references are given for each bug patterns with references to OWASP Top 10 and CWE.

Open for contributions
